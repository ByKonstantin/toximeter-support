In-App Purchases

Toximeter uses one-time consumable purchases («runs»). These purchases cannot be restored via the Restore Purchases button. If you did not receive «runs» after successful payment, please write to toximeter.info@gmail.com, specifying the date, approximate time, and Apple ID.

Issues with charges or refunds can be resolved through Apple's official service: reportaproblem.apple.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need an account? Yes, login is required via email or Apple ID.
  • How to top up «runs»? Open the purchases screen and select a suitable package.
  • What to do if the purchase didn't appear? Restart the app and contact support, attaching a screenshot of the Apple receipt.
